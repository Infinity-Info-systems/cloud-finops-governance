# Executive Cost Reporting

## Purpose

Executive cost reporting turns spend data into a decision-ready view of cost trend, exposure, and savings progress.

## What Leaders Need To See

- current spend versus forecast
- top cost drivers
- savings progress
- budget risks
- decisions required

## Reporting Rules

- keep the summary short
- connect findings to business impact
- show trend and not just snapshot
- separate facts, assumptions, and recommendations

## Executive Questions

- Is spend trending in the right direction?
- Where is the biggest variance?
- What has been fixed?
- What needs leadership action?
